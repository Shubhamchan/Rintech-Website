FSSAI REGISTRATION

01

FSSAI Registration - An Overview

  • Registration with the FSSAI is mandatory for everyone in the food business. Food processing, food manufacturing, packaging, distribution, sales, etc.
  • It would be helpful if you could register FSSAI for your business. Fssai registration/license is issued by the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI).
  • This agency oversees food businesses in accordance with the policies and regulations outlined in the FSSAI Act 2006.

02

FSSAI LICENCE REGISTRATION ELIGIBILITY

  • License and registration with FSSAI is mandatory for all food businesses. Any business that manufactures, processes, stores, transports, distributes, or sells food must obtain an FSSAI license or registration.
  • There are two types of licenses issued by the FSSAI:
  • Basic registration and State licenses.
  • Basic registration is required for small food businesses with an annual turnover of less than 12 million. All other food businesses, including those with basic registration, require a state license.
  • The eligibility criteria for the FSSAI license are: Applicant must be a food manufacturer, processor, packer, distributor, importer, or retailer of food.
  • Applicants must have a valid Food Business Certificate (FBO) from a local government or other relevant authority as required by the FSS Act 2006.
  • Applicant must have a valid registration certificate.

03

FOSCOS FSSAI LICENSE RENEWAL

  • FSSAI licenses are valid for 1-5 years and can be renewed before they expire.
  • An individual or organization holding an FSSAI registration or license may apply for license renewal by submitting an application no later than 30 days before the license expires.
  • The renewal process is similar to the application process and requires the same documents.
